Restaurants

  • Venue /
  • Restaurants

Santa Clara's vicinity has many great food options to offer. Feel free to search by type of cuisine or check out Yelp's best rated restaurants in the Santa Clara area.

San Jose is a Light Rail ride away and also has a great variety of food options to choose from. Check out Yelp's best rated restaurants in the San Jose area.

Mountain View is approximately an hour away via the VTA and has some of the best food and bars within a reasonable distance. Locals have recommended spending some evenings on Castro street. More information about taking the VTA to Mountain View can be found here.
