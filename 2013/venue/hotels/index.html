Hotel shuttle schedule

The shuttle is provided between the following hotels and the convention center. It will visit one hotel after the other and wait 10 mins at every stop.

Morning:

  • 7am: Best Western
  • 7:30am Biltmore
  • 7:45am Avatar
  • 8:00am Marriott
  • drop-off at the Santa Clara convention center: around 8:15.
  • The shuttle will do a second round, roughly 90 minutes after the first round.

Evening:

  • 5pm: Santa Clara Convention Center
  • 5:15pm: Mariott
  • 5:30pm: Avatar
  • 5:45pm: Biltmore
  • 6pm: Best Western
  • The shuttle will do a second round, roughly 90 minutes after the first round.

This shuttle will only be offered from Wednesday, March 13th through Sunday, March 17th. There will be no shuttle service during the sprints.

The Biltmore is also offering their own shuttle. Check with the hotel staff for the schedule.
