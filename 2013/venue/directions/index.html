Directions to the Venue

  • Venue /
  • Directions to the Venue

The Santa Clara Convention Center is located at 5001 Great America Pkwy, Santa Clara, Ca. We are less than 5 miles from the San Jose International Airport (SJC). Suggested methods of transportation to/from this airport would be by car, shuttle, limo/sedan, taxi, or light rail.

Rental car information from SJC.

Directions by car from SJC.

Taxi Information can be found here. A taxi ride from SJC to the Santa Clara Convention Center is estimated to cost between $17 and $27 one way.

Shuttle Information - The Hyatt Regency Santa Clara does not have a shuttle service from the SJC airport, however, there are independent shuttle companies available. The average cost for a shuttle drop off from the SJC airport to the is $21 per person.

The Santa Clara Convention Center is located approximately 45 miles from San Francisco Airport (SFO). Best methods of transportation from this airport would be by car, shuttle, or limo/sedan.
