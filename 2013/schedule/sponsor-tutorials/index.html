PyCon 2013 Sponsor Tutorials Schedule

  • Events /
  • Sponsor Tutorials

PyCon US 2013 is proud to work with our amazing sponsors to bring to you a bevy of free to attend, 1.5 hour workshops/tutorials during the PyCon tutorial days - March 13th and 14th. While Signup is not required it is encouraged so that we can get counts for snacks, food, and attendance.

We would love to have you attend these - again they are free to attend, and are provided free of charge by our amazing sponsors. Lunch and snacks will be provided and all sponsors have put a ton of work into these workshops!

Signup via the EventBrite system: http://py2013workshops.eventbrite.com/

Note: These workshops/tutorials are not the same as the regular PyCon tutorials found here: https://us.pycon.org/2013/schedule/tutorials/
