Pycon 2011 Volunteer Signup

Here's where you can match your talents & interest to a on-site volunteer role. Volunteering at Pycon could possibly be the greatest thing you will ever do. Ever!

How to volunteer

Just edit this wiki page!
Include your name and email address in the spot (below) you'd like to volunteer for.

In general, the things we're going to need people for are:

  • Registration desk volunteers
  • Swag bag stuffers
  • Session Staff
  • Tutorial day help
  • Electrical cord tapers / untapers
  • Last minute helper mob
  • Network Setup/Teardown

Session Staff

Quick signup links & current volunteer listings: Sessions.

Someone needs to say "Please be seated. Our next speaker is..."
Being a session chair really is that easy!
Session runners get speakers from the blue-room, to their session room, and assist the session chairs.
You can also sign up on the Schedule - click the "S" on the session you want to volunteer for!
