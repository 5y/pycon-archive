The PyCon 2011 Call for main conference Proposals, Tutorials, and Posters is now closed!

Thank you all for your amazing submissions - we have several hundred talks and tutorials to now review - this is an amazing number, and miles above the number of submissions we've had in the past! We also have a virtual tidal wave of "Extreme" talks.

Poster sessions are closed - we doubled the number of posters from last year, and the list of accepted posters is here. Thank you all again!

PyCon 2011 will be held March 9th through the 17th, 2011 in Atlanta, Georgia. (Home of some of the best southern food you can possibly find on Earth!) The PyCon conference days will be March 11-13, preceded by two tutorial days (March 9-10), and followed by four days of development sprints (March 14-17).
