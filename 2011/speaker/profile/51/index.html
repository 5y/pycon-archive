Speaker Profile

Christopher Perkins

As a pragmatic open source developer who values the craft of software development more than it's theory, Chris strives to do what he can to make the [web] world a better better place. With clients ranging from the National Renewable Energy Laboratory to MVP sports system, he works hard to put into action those morsels of Open Source software he develops for the greater good. Projects include Turbogears Sprox and c5t among the many projects Chris maintains at BitBucket.org
