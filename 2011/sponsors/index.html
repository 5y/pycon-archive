PyCon 2011 Sponsorship

PyCon relies on corporate sponsors to make PyCon happen every year.

PyCon 2011 will bring together an estimated 1500 Python developers and enthusiasts from a variety of backgrounds. Your sponsorship helps keep PyCon affordable and accessible to the widest possible audience - and helps you connect to the Python community.

A wide variety of sponsorships are available to match your goals and your budget. Don't see what you want? Contact us, we are flexible and willing to work with you.

With your help PyCon 2011 will be the best yet!
