Atlanta Bars

Most (virtually all) bars in the Atlanta area are also full-service restaurants, so you don't need to worry about a case of the munchies while you're out enjoying a few drinks.

Last call in the Atlanta metro area varies depending on which city you're actually in, but the ballpark is usually 3 - 4 a.m. Individual bars often close earlier, though, so it pays to check ahead.

The drinking age in Georgia is 21 years.

Places to Grab a Pint

These are some good places to grab a beer, particularly beers that are a little (or a lot) less than mainstream.

  • Brick Store Pub - At 125 East Court Square in Decatur, GA, this pub is a bit of a drive but easily accessible from MARTA. It specializes in Belgian Ales, but offers a wide variety of great beers both on tap and in the bottle.

Cocktails

If cocktails are more your speed, you may want to try one of these:

  • H Harper Station is fairly new, but it comes highly recommended if you're a fan of fine spirits or a classic cocktail. If you're longing for a Sazerac or a Vieux Carré, this might be the place for you. Plan to take a cab if you go.

Music

Are you looking for a hole in the wall with great live music? Maybe one of these is what you're looking for.

Blues

  • Northside Tavern is an institution. It has the sort of gritty feel you'd expect from a place that is consistently listed among the best Blues venues in the city. Its location is conveniently near much of the new development occurring on the west side of town, and only .4 miles from 5 Seasons Westside. There IS a cover on Friday and Saturday.
    • Maps
      • By Car
      • By MARTA
    • Hours
      • Monday-Wednesday: 4pm-2:30am
      • Thursday-Sunday 12pm-2:30am
      • MUSIC STARTS Monday-Saturday AT 10:00 PM (I don't think I've ever seen less than 3 sets, so it goes pretty late)
  • Fat Matt's Rib Shack is another legendary spot among the locals. You'll probably wind up waiting in line for some of the signature ribs, but enthusiasts will tell you they're worth it. While you can enjoy a cold beer with your food, this might be a more appropriate entry under "Restaurants".
    • Maps
      • By Car
      • By MARTA
    • Hours
      • Monday-Thursday: 11:30 - 11:30
      • Friday-Saturday: 11:30 - 12:30
      • Sunday: 1:00 - 11:30

Jazz

  • Churchill Grounds is near the historic Fox Theater and just a mile from the hotel. It is consistently ranked one of the best places in Atlanta to hear modern jazz. There is a cover and a minimum purchase (per purchase, per set) on Friday and Saturday. The first set on those nights is at 9:30p.m.
    • Maps
