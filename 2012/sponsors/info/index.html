PyCon Sponsors

PyCon relies on corporate sponsors to make PyCon happen every year.

Please note, sponsorship applications for PyCon 2012 is closed - we have to extend our thanks to the over 120 sponsors and partners signed up for PyCon 2012. If you would like to discuss participating in sponsorship for PyCon 2013 also happening in Santa Clara, please email jnoller@python.org

PyCon 2012 will bring together an estimated 1500 Python developers and enthusiasts from a variety of backgrounds. Your sponsorship helps keep PyCon affordable and accessible to the widest possible audience - and helps you connect to the Python community. It is an excellent venue for recruitment and promotion for your company.

A wide variety of sponsorships are available to match your goals and your budget. Don't see what you want? Contact us any time, we are flexible and willing to work with you to design the sponsorship package that will fulfill your business needs.

With your help PyCon 2012 will be the best yet!
