PyCon 2012 Jobs Fair Posting Guide

Posting Guidelines

If possible, postings should include the following pieces of information:

  • Job Location
  • Job Title (Optional)
  • Job Description
  • Requirements (optional)
  • About the company
    • This can be a link, or short description - this is supplemental to the information on the sponsors page)
  • What Python is used for (if not clear from the description)
  • Contact Info (name, email, website)
  • Link for your company's job portal/site
  • Telecommuting OK / No telecommuting

Supported Syntax

All Sponsor job postings support the markdown format

Example Posting

This is an example posting you can modify and copy and paste into your benefit box in your sponsor controls.

* **Location**: Washington DC (US)
* **Title**: Senior Awesome Engineer
- **Job Description**: We are looking for an experience Python programmer to work on our asynchronous and distributed backend that uses a variety of open source Python technologies such as [celery](http://celeryproject.org/), [Tornado](http://www.tornadoweb.org/) and [Postgres](http://www.postgresql.org/).
- **Requirements**
    - Must have 4+ years Python development experience
    - Must have experience with one or more Python asynchronous development frameworks
    - Web development experience a plus, but not required.
- **About the company**: Our team is growing - we currently have 30 employees, and are well funded and offer health and retirement benefits to all employees. To see our full description, visit the [sponsors page](http://us.pycon.org/2012/sponsors/).
- **What Python is used for**: Everything - from our web-based front end, to internal analytics and monitoring, and our distributed backend. We also leverage C/C++ and JavaScript heavily.
- **Contact Info**: Contact us at <jobs@acmecorp.com>, our main jobs site is located at <http://www.acemecorp.com/jobs> - take a look, we are hiring for a variety of roles.
- *Telecommuting is OK*

And the resulting output:
