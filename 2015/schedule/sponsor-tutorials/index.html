Sponsor Tutorials Schedule

  • Events /
  • Sponsor Workshops
PyCon 2015 is proud to work with our amazing sponsors to bring to you a bevy of free to attend, 1.5 hour workshops/tutorials during the PyCon tutorial days - April 8th and 9th. While Signup is not required, it is encouraged so that we can get counts for snacks, food, and attendance.
**The Sponsor Tutorials will all take place in room 513E. That room is on the 5th floor of the Palais des congres/Montreal Convention Center.**
We would love to have you attend these - again they are free to attend, and are provided free of charge by our amazing sponsors. Lunch and snacks will be provided and all sponsors have put a ton of work into these workshops! Note: These workshops/tutorials are not the same as the regular PyCon tutorials found here: [https://us.pycon.org/2015/schedule/tutorials/](https://us.pycon.org/2015/schedule/tutorials/ "")
